Competition Rules

Please read all rules carefully before participating in the INGENIOUS competition.

Eligibility

  • Teams must consist of 3-4 members from the same institution
  • All team members must be currently enrolled students

Project

  • Projects must be original work
  • The solution must be functional with a working prototype/demo

Submission Guidelines

  • All submissions must include:
    • Project proposal PPT with Poster
    • Working prototype or demo
    • Source code repository link
    • Video showcase (min 1 minute, max 2 minutes)

Winners & Prizes

  • Project Competition: 5 winners will be selected
  • Poster Competition: 3 winners will be selected
  • Video Showcase: 3 winners will be selected
  • Winners will be announced during the closing ceremony
  • Judges' decisions are final and binding

Poster Requirements

  • Poster size: A3
  • Must include project title, team members, and institution
  • Should clearly explain the problem, solution, and impact
  • Include visual elements (diagrams, charts, screenshots)

Video Guidelines

  • Duration: 1-2 minutes
  • Format: MP4 or MOV, 1080p resolution
  • Must demonstrate working prototype
  • Include brief explanation of project purpose and functionality
  • All team members should participate in the video
